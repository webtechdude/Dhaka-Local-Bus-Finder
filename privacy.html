Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 4, 2025

Dhaka Local Bus Finder ("we", "our", "us") operates the website to help users search local bus routes in Dhaka. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your information.

Information We Collect

  • Usage data: Automatic information collected when you use the site (e.g., IP address, pages visited, device and browser information, timestamps).
  • Local data stored in your browser: We store theme preference (dark/light) in Local Storage.
  • Cookies and tracking: We may use cookies and third-party services (see below).

Google AdSense and Third-Party Services

We use Google AdSense to serve ads. Google may use cookies and other identifiers to serve personalized ads based on your activity. Please review Google's Privacy & Terms for more information:

https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our service.
  • To display relevant advertising using third-party advertising networks.
  • To analyze usage and diagnose problems using analytics services.

Data Retention

We retain usage information for a limited period to help improve the site and for analytics. We do not sell your personal information.

Your Choices

You can block cookies in your browser, use private browsing, or opt-out of personalized ads via tools such as Google Ads Settings.
